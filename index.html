"Hot Economics and Cold Politics" — STM Analysis of China's Rhetoric toward Japan

GE3206 China in World Affairs · Group Research Paper

Applied Structural Topic Modeling (STM) to a corpus of 502 Chinese official texts (2014–2026) from People's Daily, Ministry of Foreign Affairs briefings, and Xinhua reports. Identified 12 latent topics — from Abenomics to Fukushima — and estimated topic prevalence trends over time to test hypotheses about economic pragmatism vs. security-driven nationalism in China's Japan discourse. Implemented full pipeline in R (jiebaR, quanteda, stm, ggplot2).
